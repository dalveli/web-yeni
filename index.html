ABOUT US

In the heart of Krakow, we’re a cozy little spot where the warmth of Turkish hospitality meets the richness of Turkish cuisine. From fresh-baked poğaças and hand-rolled böreks to traditional Turkish breakfast and homemade dishes, our menu offers a taste of Turkey at any time of the day. Whether you're starting your morning with a hearty breakfast, stopping by for lunch, or enjoying dinner with loved ones — we’re here and happy to welcome you.
